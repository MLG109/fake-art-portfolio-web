Marina López González is a digital artist specialized in 3D and illustration, also addressing fields such as pixel art focused on video games and concept art.

Graduated from the University of Design, Innovation and Technology (UDIT). Her works usually have a wide variety of styles since he considers it essential to get out of the comfort zone. Although a dark and surreal aesthetic usually predominates. Both 3D and concept art tend to have Japanese and cyberpunk influences. In contrast, the illustrations tend to convey a dreamlike sensation.
